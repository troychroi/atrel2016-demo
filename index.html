Bio

Atrel is
emerging to
represent the
next wave of pop

Atrel is emerging to represent the next wave of pop singer-songwriters in the music industry. She has worked with artists like KDrew, Taryn Manning and Project 46, and writers like 15-time BMI Award winner Kara Dioguardi and Jerry Barnes, member of Chic led by Nile Rodgers. Atrel has been living in New York City where she is currently finishing up her debut EP under Chris Leon Productions, including collaborations with Alex Suarez from Cobra Starship, Two-time Grammy winner Fran Cathcart, and more. No matter the project, Atrel delivers powerful beats, anthemic lyrics and a heart-rending performance that will leave you with something to remember.
